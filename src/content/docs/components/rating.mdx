Rating components in WeedorUI allow users to provide feedback or evaluations in the form of star or point ratings. These components are perfect for reviews, surveys, and scoring systems. Fully customizable in terms of style, size, and rating scale, WeedorUI Ratings can seamlessly adapt to any design.
